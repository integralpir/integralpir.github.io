CSS-Cascading Style Sheets,
a language for describing the appearance of web pages
written in the aforementioned markup languages, most commonly HTML.
The concept of this technology was proposed by Hakon Vium Lie,
a Norwegian scientist and information technology specialist working for the W3C consortium at the time.
